WHO WE ARE

Great converter is a tailored service that wants to help UK colleges and Universities make better decisions whenever they enrol international students in the summer. We want them to instil confidence in international students because most of them do not understand what their grades are or equivalent to when visiting new countries to study, especially in the UK. This business idea was developed on the 20th of May 2014 by three members who study in the same college (Barking and Dagenham College) namely:Roy Mugera, Daniel Jemmott and Adriana Porumb.   2. Adriana: 17 years of age, currently studying BTEC in Business Studies/Administration, Nationality-Romania.(center).

WHAT WE DO

What we do simple, we convert international grades instantly and accurately to so that colleges and Unversities may understand how international qualifications are compared to that of UK. We are doing this because we understand that it can be frustrating for the international student when they want to enroll for a course or apply for jobs in a new country and employers; universities/colleges find it difficult to interpret their qualifications. They visit various websites/other services to understand what their qualifications are worth or equivalent to but the results are unsatisfiying. Sometimes converting grades can be expensive and results of conversion may take time to arrive.

HOW WE DO

we pull together information from credible websites such as UCKAS, in order to establish a system that will enable us to compare and convert your grades instantly and accurately to match UK standards. In keeping our content relevant and consistant, we ensure that we work with UK's Ministry of Education and other Ministries involved in the Education Industry.
